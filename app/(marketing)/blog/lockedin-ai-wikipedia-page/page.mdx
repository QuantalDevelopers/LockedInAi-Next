[LockedIn AI](https://www.lockedinai.com/) is an interview copilot that uses [artificial intelligence](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Artificial_intelligence) to help users with interviews, meetings, and solving technical problems.[[3]](https://mspoweruser.com/lockedin-ai-review/) It provides real-time answers and tailored feedback while supporting various industries such as tech, finance, and marketing.

## History

LockedIn AI was founded in April 2024 to help job seekers enter the world of work by providing real-time AI interview assistance. The company's power was harnessed by the real-world challenges presented by the job market from 2022 to 2024, which saw massive layoffs and aggressive competition for jobs.

LockedIn AI is headquartered in New York City with a team including senior engineers from Amazon, CVS, and SharkNinja. The team was motivated by the struggles faced by job seekers, who often endured multiple interviews only to face rejection due to oversaturated competition and ghost job postings.

## Features

### 1. Real-Time Responsiveness

The tool gives immediate feedback to ensure conversations stay smooth and uninterrupted.[[1]](https://www.allaboutai.com/ai-reviews/lockedin-ai/) With LockedIn AI, users can be confident and prepared to solve [coding challenges](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Competitive_programming), present solutions, or answer tough questions.

### 2. Coding Copilot

LockedIn AI has a coding copilot. It helps during coding tasks, debugging, and the design of the system by providing step-by-step help. [[2]](https://www.senseicopilot.com/blog/lockedin-ai-review)

### 3. Mobile-First Design

LockedIn AI can be accessed via any browser, eliminating the need for downloads and ensuring on-the-go usability.
