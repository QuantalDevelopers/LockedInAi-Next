## Enhancing Virtual Presence with Nvidia Broadcast Eye Contact

Nvidia Broadcast Eye Contact identifies where the user is looking and replaces the lack of eye contact in real-life interactions. This software utilizes your webcam to move your eyeballs around and follow the person you speak to. In this way, a candidate can make sure they do not look away from an interviewer and can convey confidence.

## Key Features of Nvidia Broadcast Eye Contact

- **Natural Interaction:** Nvidia Broadcast Eye Contact makes presence more natural by switching the direction of where your eyes are focusing.

- **User-Friendly Setup:** The software has an easy-to-use setup. It can integrate with the most used video conferencing solutions effortlessly. In this way, you can liberate yourself from technical problems during interviews.

- **Professionalism and Engagement:** Eye contact is one of the major vital and primitive forms of non-verbal communication. Thanks to the Nvidia Broadcast Eye Contact function, you can look like you are focused and ready for an interview.

## How to Turn on The Nvidia Broacast Eye Contact Feature?

- Open the Nvidea Broadcast app.

- Click on the 'Camera' tab at the top of the application.

- Ensure your camera is selected in the 'Camera' dropdown menu. If it's not, select it from the list.

- Find the 'Eye Contact' option in the list of effects. Now turn on the Eye Contact feature shown in the image below.
