Online meetings are widely used in the contemporary work environment. Effective interactions must be well managed whether it is a sales call, a webinar, or a remote team working on a project. Using LockedIn AI, you can enhance virtual meetings by analyzing the flow of communication, suggesting immediate responses, and enhancing general performance. You will be instructed every step of the way on how to effectively use the LockedIn AI extension and app during online meetings.

## Step 1: Install the LockedIn AI Chrome Extension

The first thing the user must do before using LockedIn AI in their online meetings is install the Chrome extension:

- **Visit the Chrome Web Store:** Go to your Google Chrome, navigate to the LockedIn AI Chrome Extension page and tap on the extension icon at the top-right of the screen.

- **Click "Add to Chrome":** On the extension page, click on ‘ADD TO CHROME’ button.

- **Confirm Installation:** A pop-up will then appear in the form of a message, which will call for confirmation. Finally, click on the option labeled ‘Add Extension’ to complete the installation of the Chrome extension.

- **Pin the Extension:** You find it easily whenever you want by simply clicking right top a puzzle icon and choosing a pin icon next to LockedIn AI.

LockedIn AI has easy compatibility with major video conferencing applications like Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, and Webex:

- **Launch Your Meeting:** Launch your video conferencing software of choice and engage in the meeting as is traditional.

- **Enable LockedIn AI in Your Meeting:** After your meeting has started, click on the LockedIn AI extension icon and then share the screen of meeting. The tool is going to start working to produce the analysis of the continuous flow of the meeting as it moves along.

- **Real-Time Feedback:** While talking, LockedIn AI will give feedback regarding your pattern of communication. It will advise the interactions with your audience, the content delivery, and the persuasive tone and language on a real-time basis.

- **Instant Answer Suggestions:** In case you are offered a spin during the meeting, LockedIn AI will give suggestions of how to answer this.
