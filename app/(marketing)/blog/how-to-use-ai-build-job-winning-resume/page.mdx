## A Well-Structured, Compelling Resume

A well-structured, compelling resume is necessary to capture attention. However, in today’s job market, you also need an ATS-compliant resume to have a higher chance of reaching the company that you want to work for.

LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder makes your life easier by tailoring your resume towards specific industries, job postings, and companies based on your profile. All you need to do is provide the information, and the Resume Builder will craft a job-winning CV that’s ATS-friendly with keyword optimization.

We'll walk you through using the LockedIn AI Resume Builder, from making an account to landing that position. This guide will teach you how to employ AI suggestions, customize your options, and choose industry-specific templates to ensure your resume stands out!

## Why Choose LockedIn AI Resume Builder?

Before we embark on the step-by-step journey, let’s unravel why you need LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder:

✅ **ATS-Friendly Formatting** – The sections are tailored for Applicant Tracking Systems (ATS) compliance.

✅ **AI-Powered Content Suggestions** – Generates detailed bullet points customized for your experience and industry.

✅ **Keyword Optimization** – Increases visibility by using relevant industry keywords.

✅ **Customizable Templates** – Offers you professionally designed templates optimized for various job roles.

✅ **Real-Time Feedback** – Regularly refines content for clarity and impact.

Now, how do you maximize LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder to create an interview-winning resume?

## Step 1: Getting Started

Here are the steps to begin crafting your resume using LockedIn AI:

1️. **Sign Up or Log In** – Create an account on LockedIn AI’s website if you don’t already have one.

2️. **Go to ‘Resume Builder’** – Navigate to the resume-building tool from the dashboard.

3️. **Choose Your Resume Template** – Pick from various ATS-compliant templates designed for different industries.

💡 **Pro Tip:** If you are applying for multiple roles, tailor different versions of your resume for each position.

## Step 2: Input Your Basic Information

Once you've chosen a template, it's time to add essential personal and professional details:

✔️ **Contact Details** – Add your name, phone number, email, and LinkedIn profile.

✔️ **Professional Summary** – Provide a 2-3 sentence overview of your key skills and career/professional goals.

✔️ **Work Experience** – List previous roles, responsibilities, achievements, and awards.

💡 **AI Tip:** LockedIn AI automatically generates impactful bullet points based on your job title and industry to help you structure your experience effectively.

## Step 3: Fine-Tune Your Work Experience

The most important part of your resume is your work experience. LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder improves this section by:

✔️ **Generating AI-Suggested Bullet Points** – The AI analyzes your job title and industry to create concise, compelling bullet points.

✔️ **Enhancing Clarity and Conciseness** – Ensures each point is results-driven and impactful.

✔️ **Highlighting Achievements** – Recommends action verbs and quantifiable results to make your experience stand out.

💡 **Example:** Instead of writing, _“Managed marketing campaigns for multiple clients.”_

✅ _“Drove multi-platform marketing campaigns, resulting in a 40% increase in client engagement.”_

## Step 4: Add Keywords and Skills

Many companies use ATS to filter resumes based on relevant keywords. LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder automatically:

✔️ **Identifies Role-Specific Keywords** – Ensures your resume includes terms that recruiters and ATS systems look for.

✔️ **Suggests Relevant Skills** – The AI will recommend both hard and soft skills tailored to your industry.

💡 **Pro Tip:** Use industry-specific keywords (found in the job description) to help pass ATS scans.

## Step 5: Education & Certifications

Strengthen your resume by adding educational qualifications and certifications:

✔️ **Degrees Earned** – Include your highest level of education.

✔️ **Certifications & Courses** – List relevant professional certifications (e.g., Google Analytics, PMP, AWS Certified Developer).

✔️ **Honors & Awards** – Highlight significant academic achievements or industry recognitions.

💡 **AI Tip:** LockedIn AI’s Resume Builder can suggest industry-recognized certifications to enhance your credibility.

## Step 6: Customize Formatting for a Professional Look

Once all details are entered, LockedIn AI allows you to fine-tune the design for readability and professionalism:

✔️ **Adjust Font and Spacing** – Ensure clarity and ATS compatibility.

✔️ **Select a Clean Layout** – Stick to an easy-to-read one-page format unless you have extensive experience.

✔️ **Maintain Consistency** – Keep formatting uniform across all sections.

💡 **Pro Tip:** Avoid excessive graphics, as ATS may struggle to read highly stylized resumes.

## Step 7: Proofread, Export, and Apply!

Before submitting your resume, take these final steps:

✅ **AI-Powered Proofreading** – LockedIn AI checks for grammar, clarity, and formatting errors.

✅ **Download in Multiple Formats** – Save your resume in PDF or DOCX, depending on application requirements.

✅ **Tailor for Each Job** – Use AI suggestions to customize bullet points for specific roles.

💡 **Pro Tip:** Save a general resume template in your account and modify it for each new application.
