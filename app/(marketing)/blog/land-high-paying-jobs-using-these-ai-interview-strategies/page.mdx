## Additional Tips and Strategies for Interview Success

## Responding to AI-Generated Answers

Once LockedIn AI generates answers, it's essential to know how to present them effectively:

- If you need to pause and think about something, drink water. This short break gives you time to collect your thoughts and plan your answer.

- If you have more than one monitor, position the camera on top of the second screen. Show the answers that were produced by the AI on the second screen while focusing on the main screen to keep eye contact with the interviewer.

## Handling Unexpected Questions

- In case you come across a question that you have no idea how to handle, don’t panic. Take a deep breath and take some time to think about the question.

- When in doubt, it is always acceptable to ask the interviewer to explain or repeat a question.

- If you do not know the answer, it is fine to let the interviewer know. You can say, “I am not exactly sure about that statement, however, I am open to making adjustments where necessary.”

## Post-Interview Follow-Up

Following up after an interview is important. You can do this by:

- Thanking the interviewer after the interview and sending a follow-up thank you note within 24 hours.

- Take time to reflect on the interview. Consider what went well and what could be improved for future interviews.
